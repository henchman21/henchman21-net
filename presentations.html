IPv6. Experience Thus Far.

I have been using IPv6 for over 13 years now, since before I even had DSL Internet. My network used to have a dedicated phone line and a permanently connected dialup connection which was so stable I once had the same IP address for over 78 days. This stability complemented the HE.net tunnel I eventually setup to "get on this new internet". At the time kame.net's dancing kame was the most interesting thing available exclusively over IPv6. Now I have multi-gigabit native IPv6 connectivity, dual-homed through BGP.

Presentation available to be given with some notice.
