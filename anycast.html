Content Distribution Get Closer.

Using AnyCast technology Henchman21 gets a copy of your data closer to the user, with multiple, failure-resistant, redundant nodes.

DNS. The Core of the Internet!

Using the internet without DNS would be nearly impossible. What IP is Google at? You just asked a DNS query.

By leveraging AnyCast technology along with henchdns, Henchman21 can host one or more of your organization's DNS records.

IPv6. Supported of Course.

IPv4 exhaustion has been a topic for several years now. RIPE hit exhaustion several years ago and some new datacenters are being built IPv6 only. Henchman21 has extensive experience with IPv6, including IPv6 AnyCast.

Highly Available and Monitored to Prove it.

At Henchman21 we know that the last thing you want to be worrying about is some silly DNS issue, or why your downloads aren't available from our cluster. We monitor our service availability and often fix problems before you even become aware that there was even an issue. We will always document service outages with RFO documents and share these with our customers.
